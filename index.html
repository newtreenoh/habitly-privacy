# Habitly Privacy Policy and Account/Data Deletion Effective date: 2026-02-20 Habitly is an offline-first habit tracking app. Most features work entirely on your device. Optional paid features (such as cloud sync) use Google/Firebase services. ## Quick Links - [Summary](#summary) - [Account and Data Deletion](#account-and-data-deletion) - [Data We Process](#data-we-process) - [Where Data Is Stored](#where-data-is-stored) - [Third-Party Services](#third-party-services) - [Data Sharing](#data-sharing) - [Security](#security) - [Your Choices and Controls](#your-choices-and-controls) - [Data Retention](#data-retention) - [Children's Privacy](#childrens-privacy) - [Changes to This Policy](#changes-to-this-policy) - [Contact](#contact) ## Summary - Core habit tracking works locally on your device (no sign-in required). - Optional cloud sync (Pro feature) uses Google Sign-In and Google Firebase services to back up/sync your habit data. - We do not sell your data. - We do not use advertising SDKs. - As of the effective date of this policy, we do not use analytics/tracking SDKs in the current app build. ## Account and Data Deletion If you used Habitly cloud sync, you can delete your cloud data in-app or request account/data deletion by email. ### In-app cloud backup deletion (Pro) Habitly includes an in-app option to delete your cloud backup data: - Open Habitly - Open the Home screen menu (top-right) - Tap **Delete cloud backup** What this does: - Deletes your synced cloud backup data for the signed-in Habitly cloud sync account - Signs you out of cloud sync What this does not do: - It does not delete local data stored on your device - It does not delete your Google account itself ### Web request method (email) If you want us to delete your Habitly cloud-sync account data, email: **Email:** gpc.immerse077@passinbox.com **Subject:** Habitly account/data deletion request Please include: - The Google email address used for Habitly cloud sync - A short request (for example: "Please delete my Habitly cloud sync account and associated cloud data") - (Optional) Approximate date you used cloud sync For verified requests, we will delete: - Habitly cloud sync data associated with your account - Habitly cloud-sync sign-in/account record used for Habitly cloud sync (where applicable and under our control) What may not be deleted by us: - Local data stored on your device (you must delete this in the app, clear app storage, or uninstall) - Google Play transaction records retained by Google under Google's policies - Your Google account itself (managed by Google) Response time: - We aim to process deletion requests within 7 days. We may request basic verification to confirm the request is from the account owner. ## Data We Process ### 1) Local habit data (all users) Stored on your device: - Habit names - Habit settings (such as type, color, and options) - Habit log entries/history Purpose: - App functionality (habit tracking) ### 2) Cloud sync data (optional, Pro users only) If you choose to use cloud sync, Habitly processes: - Your Google account email address (for sign-in/account identity) - Your habit data and habit logs (user-generated content) Purpose: - App functionality (cloud backup/sync across devices) ### 3) Subscription and entitlement information Habitly uses Google Play Billing for subscriptions. The app may access purchase/entitlement status to unlock paid features. Purpose: - App functionality (subscription access) ## Where Data Is Stored ### On-device (local) Habitly stores core habit data locally on your Android device. ### Cloud (optional) If you use cloud sync, your synced data is stored using Google Firebase services for authentication and cloud data storage. ## Third-Party Services Habitly may use the following Google services for optional features: - Google Play Billing (subscriptions) - Google Sign-In / Firebase Authentication (cloud sync sign-in) - Firebase Firestore (cloud sync storage) We do not sell personal data to third parties. ## Data Sharing We do not share your personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing. Where cloud sync is used, data is processed by service providers (Google/Firebase) only as needed to provide app functionality. ## Security - Data sent between the app and cloud services is encrypted in transit. - Core local habit tracking does not require an online account. ## Your Choices and Controls ### Use Habitly without an account You can use Habitly locally without signing in. ### Optional cloud sync Cloud sync is optional. If you do not use cloud sync, your habit data remains on your device. ## Data Retention - Local data remains on your device until you delete it, clear app storage, or uninstall the app. - Cloud sync data remains until you delete it in-app or request deletion by email. - We may retain limited records if required by law or to resolve disputes or prevent abuse. ## Children's Privacy Habitly is not directed to children under 13. ## Changes to This Policy If we change this policy, we will update this page and revise the effective date. ## Contact For privacy or support requests: - gpc.immerse077@passinbox.com
