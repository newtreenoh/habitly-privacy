Habitly Privacy Policy and Account/Data Deletion

Effective date: 2026-02-20

Habitly is an offline-first habit tracking app. Core habit tracking works on your device without sign-in. Optional paid cloud sync features use Google/Firebase services.

Account and Data Deletion

If you use Habitly cloud sync, you can delete synced cloud data in-app or request account/data deletion by email.

In-app cloud backup deletion (Pro)

  • Open Habitly
  • Open the Home screen menu (top-right)
  • Tap Delete cloud backup

This deletes: your synced Habitly cloud backup data for the signed-in cloud sync account.

This does not delete: local data on your device or your Google account itself.

Web request method (email)

Email: gpc.immerse077@passinbox.com
Subject: Habitly account/data deletion request

Please include the Google email used for Habitly cloud sync.

For verified requests, we will delete:

  • Habitly cloud sync data associated with your account
  • The Habitly cloud-sync account record under our control (where applicable)

What may not be deleted by us:

  • Local data stored on your device (delete in-app / clear app storage / uninstall)
  • Google Play transaction records retained by Google under its policies
  • Your Google account itself (managed by Google)

We aim to process verified deletion requests as quickly as possible, and no later than 30 days, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

Data We Process

  • Local habit data (habit names, settings, and log entries) for app functionality
  • Cloud sync data (optional): Google account email (for sign-in/account identity) and habit/log data for cloud backup/sync
  • Subscription/entitlement information to unlock paid features

Third-Party Services

  • Google Play Billing (subscriptions)
  • Google/Firebase services for optional cloud sync and sign-in

We do not sell personal data. We do not share personal data for advertising or marketing.

Security

  • Data sent between the app and cloud services is encrypted in transit.
  • Core local habit tracking does not require an online account.

Data Retention

  • Local data remains on your device until you delete it, clear app storage, or uninstall the app.
  • Cloud sync data remains until deleted in-app or by verified deletion request.
  • We may retain limited records where required by law or to resolve disputes/prevent abuse.
